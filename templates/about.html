Frequently Asked Questions
How many ratings and "1-on-1 booze battles" are needed to make Glass work?

You can stop at any time. Glass learns from your ratings and "1-on-1 battles" to read your preferences accurately. So, tap, tap, tap, whenever you can.


What is your drink of choice?

When are you planning to release the completed version of Glass?

We are working and drinking very hard to release the official version. We expect to have it ready in 2015.


Which versions of OS is Glass compatible with?

For iOS, you need at least iOS8, and for Android, you can check it now by visiting Google Play store.


Are recommendations accurate?

Yes. They are undoubtedly accurate. We developed the most accurate recommendation engine in the world by using rating data and comparison data. This means that you can contribute to make it even better by being a member of the Glass society.
